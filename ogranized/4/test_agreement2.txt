Document 2: Lease Agreement

This Lease Agreement (“Lease”) is made and entered into on this 1st day of August, 2025, by and between Greenfield Properties LLC (“Landlord”), whose address is 789 Elm Avenue, Seattle, Washington, and Michael B. Johnson (“Tenant”), residing at 234 Oak Street, Seattle, Washington.

Premises. Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the residential property located at 1010 Pine Street, Apartment 5B, Seattle, Washington (“Premises”).

Term. The term of this Lease shall commence on September 1, 2025, and shall continue for a period of twelve (12) months, ending on August 31, 2026.

Rent. Tenant agrees to pay Landlord monthly rent of $1,500, payable in advance on the first day of each month.

Security Deposit. Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $1,500 upon execution of this Lease. The security deposit shall be held to cover damages or unpaid rent and will be returned to Tenant within thirty (30) days after termination of this Lease, less any deductions.

Use of Premises. The Premises shall be used solely for residential purposes and occupied only by Tenant and Tenant’s immediate family.

Maintenance and Repairs. Tenant agrees to maintain the Premises in a clean and safe condition and shall notify Landlord promptly of any necessary repairs.

Utilities. Tenant shall be responsible for payment of all utilities, including electricity, water, gas, and internet services.

Termination. Either party may terminate this Lease at the end of the term by providing at least thirty (30) days written notice. Early termination may be subject to penalties as specified by applicable law.

Governing Law. This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State of Washington.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Lease Agreement as of the date first above written.

Michael B. Johnson, Tenant

Emily R. Green, Manager, Greenfield Properties LLC
